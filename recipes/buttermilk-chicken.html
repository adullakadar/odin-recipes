The Best Air-Fried Buttermilk Chicken (On Everything)

Crispy fried chicken mate you'll love it. You don't have to use breadcrumbs even come on man.

Here are the ingredients you need

  • 3 Chicken Thigh Cuts
  • 100ml of your choice of Buttermilk (or laban)
  • Turmeric Powder
  • Your choice of hot sauces, but primarily sriracha and buffalo sauce
  • Paprika, Cayenne, Garlic Powder, Cumin, Onion Powder, Cajun, Hot chilli powder
  • Vinegar
  • Breadcrumbs / Panko

Steps

  1. Clean and rinse your chicken with some turmeric and rinse. Let it dry for like 15 minutes.
  2. Get a container and drop allat buttermilk in. Throw in a generous amount of seasoning, hot sauces and a tasteful amount of vinegar.
  3. Mix well until it becomes a nice shade of orange.
  4. Thro yo chicken in that buttermilk and throw it in the fridge for at leaast 8 hours.
  5. Once marinaded, take yo chicken and you can do one of two things:

    6. Oil-Fried Chicken

    1. Pour some bread crumbs and spices in a bowl and mix well.
    2. Place chicken into breadcrumbs and cover well.
    3. Heat up a deep pan with some cooking oil and thro yo chicken in for 5 minutes. Flip and cook for another 5 minutes.
    4. Remove chicken and place on a drying rack for another 5 minutes.
    5. Plate that shi and you got yourself some damn good fried chicken.

    Air-Fried Chicken

    1. Place your chicken in an air fryer (double foil the bottom). Get a tiny cup kinda thing and fill it with some water, enough to steam the chicken when it starts boiling.
    2. Turn on the air fryer and flip when it recommends you to.
    3. After its done cooking, plate that shi and you got yourself some damn good baked chicken.
