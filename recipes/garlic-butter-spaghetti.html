The Simplest Garlic Butter Spaghetti

Guaranteed to have you full for the day AND night

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti
  • Butter
  • Garlic
  • Onions
  • Mushrooms
  • Chilli flakes, garlic powder, oregano, salt and pepper
  • Olive oil
  • Cheese (Mozzarella or parmesan)

Steps

  1. Boil water in a pot. Add salt and olive oil half way to enhance boiling. Once boiling, add spaghetti and leave for 5 minutes.
  2. In the mean time, dice up some onions and garlic.
  3. To a deep pan, low heat and add olive oil.
  4. Your spaghetti should be done by now. Reserve quarter cup of pasta water and drain the spaghet.
  5. Once at low heat, add garlic and onions. While stirring, add butter, garlic powder, oregano and chilli flakes.
  6. Add spaghetti and a bit of the pasta water. (DON'T ADD TOO MUCH)
  7. Mix well and throw in some salt and pepper.
  8. Kill the heat and add some cheese. Mix well
  9. Plate that shi in portions and you got some good carbs right there.
